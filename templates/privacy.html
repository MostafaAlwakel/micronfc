Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create a MicroNFC account, we collect the following information:

  • Account Info: Your name, email address, and password (stored encrypted).
  • Profile Info: Job title, bio, phone number, social media links, and profile/cover photos.
  • Medical Info: Blood type, allergies, medications, chronic diseases, and emergency contacts — only if you choose to enter them.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics like page visits and NFC tap counts to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and smart profile.
  • To display your profile when your NFC card or bracelet is tapped.
  • To power your AI assistant using the context you provide.
  • To send account-related emails (verification, security alerts).
  • To improve and maintain the platform.

3. Medical Data

Medical information you enter is treated with the highest level of care. It is only displayed on your public medical profile page when accessed through your NFC bracelet. You have full control over what you enter — you can update or delete any medical information at any time from your dashboard. We do not sell, share, or use your medical data for any purpose other than displaying it on your profile.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with:

  • Cloudinary: For storing and serving your profile and portfolio images.
  • Resend: For sending transactional emails (verification, notifications).
  • AI Provider: Your AI assistant context is sent to our AI provider only when you or a visitor interacts with the chatbot.

5. Your Rights & Control

  • You can update or delete any information in your profile at any time.
  • You can request full account deletion by contacting us at support@micronfc.info.
  • You control what data is visible on your public profile.
  • You can deactivate your card(s) at any time from your dashboard.

6. Security

We take security seriously. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plaintext. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Profile pages linked to NFC cards are protected by unique, cryptographically generated codes. Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password.

7. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Session data is cleared when you log out or close your browser.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out:

Contact Form support@micronfc.info
